Exotics Jr. Suites

For the tribal in you.

This is for the adventurous; if one Dreams of having a Romantic Escape you will be Spoiled with all the Indulgences in these Jr Suites and the Exotic in you will be Satisfied. Our spacious Exotic Junior Suites feature rich, earthy tones and subtle African-inspired décor, including artisanal masks, carved accents, and patterned details. Nestled among the trees with views of Galeairy Lake, these suites offer a warm, immersive retreat. Each includes a double walk-in shower with a Turkish steam bath option for added relaxation.

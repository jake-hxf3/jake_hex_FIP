Summer Vacation at a 5 Star Resort

To do a lot or do nothing at all? A tough decision when almost everything is included in one package. No matter what time of year, there's always a lot to do at Couples Resort.

Then again, who said you couldn't just relax in a deep padded chaise lounge on your own room deck and indulge in a favorite novel while resting in the soothing warmth of the day. Whatever you choose, you're sure to enjoy all of our activities.

Find Your Moment in Nature

The resort sits on the semi-private Galeairy Lake, an 11 km stretch reaching into Algonquin Park, with 95% of its shoreline untouched—perfect for exploring quiet bays and natural scenery.

The rugged surroundings invite guided trail rides into the Algonquin wilderness, while direct access to 35 km of trails along the Madawaska River offers hours of mountain biking or ATVing. You can also enjoy a peaceful hike on the resort’s own trails.

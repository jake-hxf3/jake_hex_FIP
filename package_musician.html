Musicians - Play & Stay

Calling Skilled Musicians for a unique opportunity to share your talent at a 5-Star resort in exchange for an all-inclusive getaway. Do you have a skill that a 5-Star Resort such as ours could benefit from? We might have something to barter. There is a saying: “it never hurts to ask (politely)”.

How The Program Works

  • Perform solo, as a duo, or in a small band.
  • Play in our Fine Dining Room (Jazz, Easy Listening, Romantic, Blues, Light Rock, Country, Classical).
  • Evening performance: 6:00 pm – 11:00 pm (with breaks).
  • Instruments: stand-up piano provided; bring your own strings/winds.

Play & Stay Packages

Pre-booking is possible. Final confirmation happens within 7 days of your performance date (availability-dependent).
All packages are all-inclusive, based on availability.

  • 2-Night Stay → Perform 1 night
  • 3-Night Stay → Perform 2 nights
  • Midweek 4-Night Stay → Perform 2 nights

How to Apply

  • Send your email to: info@couplesresort.ca
  • Required: a video of your performance (not audio-only)
  • We also need your Facebook & Instagram links for promotion.
  • After your stay, post a review with photos or video on your social media.
